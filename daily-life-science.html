Cooking involves chemistry, heat transfer and physical changes.

Refrigeration uses scientific principles to preserve food.

💡 Light Bulbs

Electricity is converted into light energy.

🧺 Washing Machines

Mechanical engineering and chemistry work together to clean clothes.

Combine physics, electronics, chemistry and computing.

Uses communication technology and data transmission systems.

🛰 GPS

Satellites help determine locations anywhere on Earth.

💻 Computers

Powered by semiconductor technology and advanced engineering.

💉 Vaccines

Protect people from infectious diseases.

🩺 Medical Imaging

X-rays, MRI and ultrasound help doctors diagnose diseases.

💊 Medicines

Developed through scientific research and testing.

🧬 Genetics

Helps understand inherited traits and diseases.

From the moment we wake up until we sleep, science influences almost everything we do.
