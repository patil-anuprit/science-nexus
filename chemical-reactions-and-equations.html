📖 Chapter Introduction

Everything around us undergoes change. Milk turns into curd. Iron rusts. Food gets digested. Wood burns. These changes involve chemical reactions. A chemical reaction is a process in which one or more substances are converted into new substances having different properties. Chemical reactions are responsible for countless natural and man-made processes occurring around us every day.

⭐ Important Definitions

Chemical Reaction

A process in which one or more substances are transformed into new substances with different properties.

Reactants

The substances that take part in a chemical reaction.

Products

The new substances formed after a chemical reaction.

Chemical Equation

A symbolic representation of a chemical reaction using chemical formulae.
