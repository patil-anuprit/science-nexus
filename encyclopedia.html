📚 Science Encyclopedia

An A–Z collection of important scientific concepts.

A – F

A – Atom

The basic unit of matter.

B – Bacteria

Microscopic living organisms found everywhere.

C – Cell

The basic structural and functional unit of life.

D – DNA

The molecule carrying genetic information.

E – Energy

The ability to do work.

F – Force

A push or pull acting on an object.

G – L

G – Gravity

The force attracting objects toward each other.

H – Habitat

The natural home of an organism.

I – Inertia

The tendency of an object to resist changes in motion.

J – Joule

The SI unit of energy.

K – Kinetic Energy

Energy possessed by a moving object.

L – Light

Electromagnetic radiation visible to the human eye.
