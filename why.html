Popular Science Questions

Why is the Sky Blue?

Sunlight contains many colours. Blue light is scattered more by Earth's atmosphere, making the sky appear blue.

Why Do Stars Twinkle?

Earth's atmosphere bends starlight slightly, causing stars to appear to twinkle.

Why is the Sea Salty?

Rivers carry dissolved minerals into oceans over millions of years, making seawater salty.

Why Do Onions Make Us Cry?

Cutting onions releases chemicals that irritate our eyes and cause tears.

Why Does Ice Float?

Ice is less dense than liquid water, so it floats.

Why Do Magnets Attract Iron?

Iron contains magnetic domains that align with magnetic fields.
