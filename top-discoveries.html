🏆 The Greatest Scientific Discoveries Ever Made

⚛ Atom

Understanding atoms revealed the building blocks of matter and transformed modern science.

🧬 DNA

The discovery of DNA explained heredity and revolutionized biology and medicine.

🌍 Gravity

Gravity explains why planets orbit stars and why objects fall to Earth.

⚡ Electricity

Electricity powers homes, industries, computers and modern civilization.

💉 Vaccines

Vaccines have saved millions of lives by preventing deadly diseases.

🔬 Germ Theory

Scientists discovered that microorganisms cause many diseases.

📡 Radio Waves

Enabled radio, television, mobile phones and wireless communication.

🚀 Space Exploration

Humanity reached the Moon and explored distant worlds.

🧪 Periodic Table

Organized all known chemical elements into a powerful scientific framework.

🧠 Neurons

Understanding the nervous system revealed how the brain functions.

🌱 Photosynthesis

Explained how plants convert sunlight into food and support life on Earth.

💻 Semiconductors

Made computers, smartphones and modern electronics possible.
