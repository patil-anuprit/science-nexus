⚛ Physics Legends

Isaac Newton

Developed the laws of motion and universal gravitation.

Albert Einstein

Developed the theory of relativity and changed modern physics.

Nikola Tesla

Pioneer of alternating current and electrical innovation.

🧪 Chemistry Legends

Marie Curie

Discovered radioactivity and won two Nobel Prizes.

Dmitri Mendeleev

Created the foundation of the modern periodic table.

🇮🇳 Indian Science Legends

C. V. Raman

Discovered the Raman Effect and won the Nobel Prize in Physics.

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Scientist, aerospace engineer and former President of India.

Vikram Sarabhai

Father of the Indian space programme.

Homi Bhabha

Pioneer of India's nuclear science programme.
