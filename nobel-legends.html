🌟 Nobel Prize Giants

Marie Curie

The first person to win two Nobel Prizes and a pioneer of radioactivity.

C. V. Raman

Won the Nobel Prize in Physics for discovering the Raman Effect.

Albert Einstein

Received the Nobel Prize for explaining the photoelectric effect.

Alexander Fleming

Discovered penicillin, leading to the antibiotic revolution.

Subrahmanyan Chandrasekhar

Made major contributions to astrophysics and stellar evolution.

Har Gobind Khorana

Helped explain how genetic information is encoded and expressed.

Abdus Salam

Made important contributions to theoretical physics.

Venkatraman Ramakrishnan

Won the Nobel Prize for studies of the ribosome.

🌠 A Nobel Lesson

Great discoveries begin with curiosity, grow through hard work, and change the world through knowledge.
