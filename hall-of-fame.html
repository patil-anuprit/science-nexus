🌟 Legendary Scientists

Albert Einstein

Developed the Theory of Relativity and changed modern physics forever.

Isaac Newton

Formulated the laws of motion and universal gravitation.

Marie Curie

Pioneer of radioactivity and the first person to win two Nobel Prizes.

Galileo Galilei

Known as the father of modern observational astronomy.

Nikola Tesla

Revolutionized electricity and alternating current systems.

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Indian aerospace scientist and former President of India.

C. V. Raman

Discovered the Raman Effect and won the Nobel Prize in Physics.

Charles Darwin

Developed the theory of evolution by natural selection.

Gregor Mendel

Known as the father of genetics.

Louis Pasteur

Made groundbreaking contributions to microbiology and vaccines.

Rosalind Franklin

Her work was crucial in understanding the structure of DNA.

Stephen Hawking

Expanded our understanding of black holes and cosmology.

🧠 What Makes a Great Scientist?

Curiosity. Observation. Creativity. Persistence. Every great discovery began with someone asking: "Why?"
